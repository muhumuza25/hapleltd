Our Mission
To mobilize communities, seek partnerships and financial support to sensitize the communities about the value of education, promote vocational skills and sciences in order to produce global and world class labor for Uganda, Africa and the world.
Our Vision
To contribute to the promotion of education in Africa and the world at large, by fostering strategic partnerships and models that will produce excellent graduates, job creators with an international mindset and globallabor force.
