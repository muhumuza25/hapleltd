SCHOOL FEES LOAN PROGRAM

International/National and Local Schools
Partnership Program

Haple International Ltd, seeks through this program to connect schools in Uganda, EastAfrica, Africa and the world. Partnerships like these are very important to give our pupils and students a wider understanding of the communities and environment they live in and this will prepare their mindsets for a huge task ahead of them, as labor in preparation.
Kindergarten, primary, secondary, vocational and technical schools,Universities with an international syllabus.

More than just helping

Haple provides school fees to parents without interest and this money is paid by the organization directly to the school.Pay school fees in affordable installments as low as 1% of total fees per day..
